✓ Clear Space

Always maintain minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "C" character.

✓ Color Usage

The "Algo" portion should always use the accent color (#ffd8d8) on dark backgrounds or alert color (#ed3500) on light backgrounds.

✗ No Modifications

Do not stretch, rotate, apply effects, or change the colors beyond the approved variants.

✗ Minimum Size

The wordmark should never appear smaller than 100px wide to maintain legibility.
